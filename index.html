TOP 5 STRATEGIC PRIORITIES

1. Platform Focus

  • Instagram + TikTok receive 80% of effort
  • Instagram: Strengthen existing 1,443 followers
  • TikTok: Lowest CAC, highest virality potential
  • Other platforms: Minimal effort, maximum repurposing

2. Content Efficiency

  • Create once, distribute everywhere model
  • 1 Saturday walk = 20+ pieces of content
  • 5 content pillars: Trails (30%), Tips (25%), Community (25%), Challenges (15%), Events (5%)
  • Weekly time: 12-15 hours total

3. Paid Advertising

  • Instagram: 3 campaigns (awareness, conversion, retargeting)
  • TikTok: Discovery push (KES 1,500/week x 7 weeks)
  • Expected: 950 followers from ads alone
  • CPM: KES 200-600 depending on platform

4. Influencer Partnerships

  • 3 nano influencers (2K-8K followers each)
  • KES 3,000 per influencer
  • Deliverables: 3 Stories + 1 Reel + 1 TikTok + event attendance
  • Expected: 300-450 followers, high-quality UGC

5. Community Building

  • Saturday walks as content goldmine
  • 30-day step challenges for engagement
  • Email list for retention (bi-weekly newsletters)
  • UGC campaign: 100+ member posts/month by Week 10

6. Measurement & Optimization

  • Daily: Story engagement, comment sentiment
  • Weekly: Follower growth, top posts, engagement trends
  • Monthly: ROI analysis, A/B test results, pivots
  • 8 A/B tests planned across 12 weeks
